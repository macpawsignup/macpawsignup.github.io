Introduction

A MacPaw Account is the easiest way to manage all your MacPaw products from one secure dashboard. Whether you use CleanMyMac, Setapp, Gemini, The Unarchiver, or other MacPaw applications, creating an account allows you to keep your licenses, subscriptions, downloads, and billing information organized.

If you're purchasing a MacPaw product for the first time, you'll typically be prompted to create an account during the checkout process. Even if you already own MacPaw software, having an account makes reinstalling applications, managing subscriptions, and accessing support much easier.

The good news is that signing up only takes a few minutes. Once your account is ready, you'll have access to your purchases from any supported device simply by signing in.

Why Create a MacPaw Account?

Creating an account offers far more than simply storing your license key. It gives you complete control over your MacPaw products and helps simplify software management.

  • Access all purchased applications from one place.
  • Manage subscriptions and renewals.
  • Download the latest versions of your software.
  • View your purchase history.
  • Recover your licenses if you reinstall macOS.
  • Update billing information securely.
  • Contact MacPaw Support more efficiently.
  • Manage multiple devices using one account.

Before You Begin

Before creating your account, make sure you have the following:

  • A valid email address.
  • A secure password.
  • A reliable internet connection.
  • A supported web browser.
  • A few minutes to complete the registration.

How to Sign Up for a MacPaw Account

Step 1: Visit the Official Website

Open your preferred web browser and visit the official MacPaw website. From the homepage, navigate to the account or sign-up section where new users can register.

Step 2: Click Create Account

Locate the Create Account or Sign Up button. Clicking this option opens the registration form.

Step 3: Enter Your Email Address

Type a valid email address that you regularly use. This address will become your login ID and will also be used for account verification, password recovery, and subscription notifications.

Step 4: Create a Strong Password

Choose a password that is difficult to guess. A strong password usually contains uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

Step 5: Review the Terms

Read the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy before proceeding. Accept the terms by checking the required box.

Step 6: Verify Your Email

MacPaw will send a verification email to your registered email address. Open your inbox and click the verification link to activate your account.

Step 7: Log In

Once your account has been verified, return to the login page and sign in using your email address and password.

Common Sign-Up Problems

Verification Email Didn't Arrive

Check your Spam or Junk folder. If you still don't receive the message, request another verification email from the sign-up page.

Email Already Exists

If your email address has already been registered, simply use the password recovery option instead of creating another account.

Weak Password

Create a password that combines letters, numbers, and symbols to improve security.

Registration Won't Complete

Refresh your browser, clear cached files, or try another browser before attempting the registration again.

Tips to Keep Your Account Secure

  • Use a unique password.
  • Never share your login credentials.
  • Update your recovery email if necessary.
  • Review your subscription details regularly.
  • Log out after using a shared computer.
  • Keep your browser updated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a MacPaw Account free?

Yes. Creating a MacPaw Account is completely free.

Can I manage multiple products?

Yes. One account allows you to manage all supported MacPaw products.

Can I change my email address later?

Yes. You can update your account information after logging in.

What happens if I forget my password?

Use the Forgot Password option on the login page to securely reset your password.
